Headliners

Arctic Monkeys

The British indie rock band Arctic Monkeys has finally returned to the festival stages. They will make a great act of it so make sure you don’t miss them! Listen here to one of their most famous songs.

Kings of Leon

The 3 brothers of Kings of Leon have worked very hard the last few years. They have already released 7 albums. There act will be amazing!

Hardwell

Hardwell has played a number of festivals including Starlite Marbella, Kingsland Festival Amsterdam, Ultra South Africa - Cape Town, Weekend Festival Baltic, Ultra South Africa - Johannesburg and Summerburst - Göteborg.

Nicky Romero

Nicky Romero has played a number of festivals including DLDK: Don't Let Daddy Know Amsterdam, Kingsland Festival Groningen, Medusa Sunbeach Festival, Kingsland Festival Twente, Sensation Spain and Leiria Dancefloor.

David Guetta

David Guetta is undeniably one of the world's biggest DJ and producers, having helped define an entire generation of dance music.

G-Easy

G-Eazy is a rapper, producer and songwriter hailing from United States. Since his debut in 2006 he has been one of the most prolific rappers of his generation, releasing three studio albums, six mixtapes and three E.P’s.

Calvin Harris

The first British solo artist to reach one billion streams on Spotify first made his name back in 2007 with 'Acceptable in the 80s', when Harris himself performed as the centre-piece and vocalist of his act.

Nick Cave & the Bad Seeds

Nick Cave has played a number of festivals including INmusic Festival, Rock Werchter, APE Presents and Primavera Sound.

Guns N´ Roses

Guns N’ Roses will be returning to Europe in the Summer of 2022 and continue their World Tour to South America, Asia, Australia and New Zealand in the Fall.

Sir Elton John

Elton John has played a number of festivals including Musilac and Les Déferlantes Sud de France.

Dua Lipa

A regular on the summer festival circuit, impressive appearances at Reading and Leeds Festival, Sziget, and Mad Cool Festival demonstrated that as one of the world’s most engaging performers, she knows how to work a festival crowd.

Boris Brejcha

Boris Brejcha has played a number of festivals including Contact Festival, FCKNYE Festival, World Club Dome, LX Music presents Boris Brejcha, Isle of Summer Open Air | Olympia Reitanlage and Electronic Sensation.
